OUR STORY

About Infigo Holdings

Delivering modern, customized, and scalable solutions tailored for residential, commercial, and large-scale development projects across Sri Lanka.

Our Foundation

Building Lasting Value Through Quality

Headquartered in Colombo 02, Infigo Holdings (Pvt) Ltd is a dynamic Sri Lankan furniture and interior design solutions provider. Since our inception in 2019, Infigo has grown steadily by focusing on what matters most—quality craftsmanship, functional design, customer-centric solutions, and value for money.

We serve a diverse client base by combining engineering precision, design aesthetics, and project execution excellence to meet evolving customer expectations across Sri Lanka. Our product portfolio spans office, home, industrial, and bespoke furniture, supported by innovative design thinking and environmentally responsible practices.
